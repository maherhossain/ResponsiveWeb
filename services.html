PVC Banner

PVC Banners are our specialty. We offer a fast and reliable service with no restriction on width or length. All our banners are printed at high quality 720dpi on heavy weight 520gsm fire rated PVC banner material. Banners are supplied with reinforced folded hems, secured with heavy duty eyelets every 24". Because we print all our banners "in house" you can be sure you are buying at the most competitive rates.

Product Specification:

520gsm vinyl PVC banner thickness. Fire retardant to din75200 (class B) – Suitable for indoors and outdoors. Very durable – scratch, tear, water and fade resistant – vinyl PVC banner material life of 7 years. A bright white, smooth satin surface for an exceptional quality finish. Printed with premium eco-friendly solvent ink which is waterproof, hard wearing, scratch resistant and fade resistant for the life of the vinyl PVC banner material. 9mm brass eyelets every 2ft and hemming as standard (other options available on request).High resolution (1440dpi) full-colour printing for vibrant colour and clarity on your vinyl banner sign

Fabric/Cloth Banner

We offer a wide range of coated textile cloth banners and poly woven fabric banners. These custom banners are light weight, flexible and eliminate reflection problems. The fabric textile surfaces are well suited for small to large upscale interior images. The poly materials can tackle outdoor applications with style. Soft indoor banners can be draped, hung from the ceiling, or used with a frame display system to make distinctive visuals. Fabric banners provide a touch of style and elegance that will definitely gain the attention of your target audience.

Product Specification:

Printed on 5 oz. to 11 oz. Cloth banner materials. Print up to 6 colors. Light to heavy weave available. Fade resistant solvent and UV based inks. Complete finishing capabilities: hems, pole pockets, grommets, webbing, D-rings/O-rings and hot knifing.

Clear print

Fine print,small print, or "mouseprint" is less noticeable print smaller than the more obvious larger print it accompanies that advertises or otherwise describes or partially describes a commercial product or service.The larger print that is used in conjunction with fine print by the merchant often has the effect of deceiving the consumer into believing the offer is more advantageous than it really is, via a legal technicality which requires full disclosure of all terms or conditions, but does not specify the manner of disclosure. There is also strong evidence to suggest that the fine print is not read by the majority of consumers.
